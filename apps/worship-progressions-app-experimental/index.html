Flowset analyzes chord progressions using the Nashville Number System to find songs with similar harmonic movement. You can search with Nashville numbers or plain chord letters, and Flowset will normalize the progression across keys before matching sections like chorus, verse, or bridge.

What these search terms mean

Strict sequence: Your progression must appear in order. Slash chords are normalized (for example, 1/3 can match 1).

Balanced: Finds in-order matches and close in-order variants with full progression coverage.

Flexible: Like Balanced, but treats slash chords (for example, 1/3) as their base chord (1).

Slash chord: A chord over a bass note, like 1/3.
